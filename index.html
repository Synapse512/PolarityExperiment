Information

Test Instructions:

Begin the test by clicking the begin test button. You will be given 2 articles to read, both are 400 words, and contain 5 questions each. Each will also have a different polarity, or light setting (white and black). Once you get to the end of each article, click the "Finish Reading" button, and answer the 5 questions. Once you have done this for both articles, you will be provided your results. Copy them, and paste them into the results section on the Google form.



Short Instructions:

1: Click begin test button

2: Read 1st article, click "Finish Reading" button

3: Answer the 5 questions, and submit them

4: Repeat all steps for 2nd article

5: Results screen will let you copy your data, copy and paste it into the Google Form, and please submit



Privacy:

Through this application, no personal data is saved, nor has the capability included to save data. The application locally records all statistics throughout the experiment. Once the experiment has been completed, the results will not be shared unless explicitly submitted through the Google Form provided. The Google Form does not save any data except for the submitted results. You maintain the right to withdraw from the experiment at any time, whether before, during, or after you have participated in it, with no added consequence. All submitted data will be strictly used for academic purposes. The entire application is open sourced in interest of transparency, and is publicly available at: https://github.com/Synapse512/PolarityExperiment
